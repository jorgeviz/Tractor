Situation

Some of the most fertile regions in the world for agricultural production exist in Latin America, yet only around 30% of the usable land has been developed for crop production. This is due, in part, to the economic situation in rural areas where more than 90 million people live in poverty.

Source: FAO

Solution

We connect individuals interested in social online investment with farmers in developing regions, in order to transform agricultural productivity through the application of technology and training programs. We guarantee a sustainable end-to-end process.

  • Learn More

Advantages

Domain Expertise

To ensure productivity increase, Tractor brings the domain knowledge expert directly to the region to implement the most suitable technologies for their given plantation, on a case-by-case basis.

Training

We perform a continuous-improvement training program with the farmers to allow them to resume the same practices by themselves at the end of their cycle with Tractor.

Risk-free

We provide a complete platform for farmers, where they can take advantage of their land without the financial risk and complicated process of financial institutions.

Scalable

We connect individuals interested in social online investment with farmers in developing regions, with transparent traceability of everyone's funds and a direct contact with the projects' progress and impact.

Secure

Every relevant transaction is stored in a distributed ledger, allowing any party (farmer, social-investor, volunteer, etc.) be able to ensure every step of the agricultural process and each cent is correctly managed and reaches the right people.
